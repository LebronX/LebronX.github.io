About

I am a second year PhD. student with the supervision of Dr. Daniel Neider at the Max Planck Institute for Software Systems. I obtain my bachelor degree in the School of Data and Computer Science from Sun Yat-sen University. I have unforgettable visting experience at HKUST and SUSTECH under the supervision of Prof. Shing-Chi CHEUNG, Prof. Yepang Liu and Dr. Ming Wen in the CASTLE Group. My research interests include Verification of machine learning models, Automata learning, and Software Testing.
