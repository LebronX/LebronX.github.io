About

I am a Ph.D. student in University of Alberta, with the supervision of Dr. Lei Ma. I got my master degree under the supervision of Dr. Daniel Neider at the Max Planck Institute for Software Systems, Kaiserslautern, Germany. I obtain my bachelor degree in the School of Data and Computer Science from Sun Yat-sen University. I have unforgettable visting experience at HKUST and SUSTECH under the supervision of Prof. Shing-Chi CHEUNG, Prof. Yepang Liu and Dr. Ming Wen in the CASTLE Group. My research interests include Verification of machine learning models, Automata learning, and Software Testing.
